Welcome to the Club.

What We Do.

We provide repair, restoration, and custom fabrication services of the highest caliber for vintage motorcycles, for customers of the Merrimack Valley and beyond.

Our Specialty.

Our specialty lies in the restoration and custom design of cafe-racer styled motorcycles. However, we pride ourselves on versatility, and can deliver other types of custom builds at the request of the customer.

Our Partners.

We also bring to the table several industry-leading partners to ensure our customers receive the highest quality results in every department.

Partners Include.

  • All Tuned Up Garage of Merrimack NH
  • R&R Cycle Engines of Manchester NH
  • Stone's Custom of Northborough MA
  • Coker Vintage Motorcycle Tires
